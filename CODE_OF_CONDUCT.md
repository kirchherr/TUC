# Code Of Conduct

TUC should be a technically serious and welcoming project.

Participants are expected to:

- Treat contributors with respect.
- Critique ideas and code, not people.
- Keep discussions focused, specific, and actionable.
- Assume good intent while staying precise about technical risk.
- Avoid harassment, abuse, or exclusionary behavior.

Maintainers may moderate issues, pull requests, discussions, and other project
spaces to protect the quality and safety of the project.
